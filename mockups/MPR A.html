I. SCHEDULING

To make a reservation request, please follow the instructions listed below:

  1. Visit csueastbay.edu/uu
  2. Click on "Reservations" from the red tab
  3. Click on "Reservation Form" which should lead you to our online Reservation Request Form submission page
  4. Upon completion of the request form, click "Submit" and we will receive an email indicating that you've requested a room in the university union.

II. RESERVATION ROOM HOURS

Room hours coincide with the building hours. Should your event begin before or extend after building hours, please note that a non-operating fee will be incurred (Please refer to our Pricing page).

​
Regular Semester  Monday-Thursday: 7AM-10PM Friday: 7AM-7PM Weekend: CLOSED
Summer Monday-Friday: 8AM-5PM Weekend: CLOSED
Academic Recess Monday-Friday: 8AM-5PM Weekend: CLOSED

 

III. EQUIPMENT

You may request for the following to be included in your room reservation request:

  • Cocktail Tables
  • Laptops
  • Podium
  • Tables
  • LCD Projector and Screen
  • P.A. System with 1 microphone
  • White Board
  • Flip Chart Easel with Markers

*If you anticipate outside vendors performing services, such as food or entertainment, be aware that the University Union requires specific insurance coverage. Please see the UU reservation office for more information.

IV. CLEARING ROOM

You are allowed in the room 30 minutes prior to your event to set up and 30 minutes after your event to clean up. Please respect this space and ensure that the room is left neat and tidy.
